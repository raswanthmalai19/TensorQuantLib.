## Benchmark 1: Compression vs Tolerance (3-asset, 30 pts/axis)

Full grid: `(30, 30, 30)` = 27,000 entries, 210.9 KB

| Tolerance | Max Rank | TT KB | Ratio | Error | SVD ms |
|-----------|----------|-------|-------|-------|--------|
| 1e-01 | 2 | 1.9 | 112.5× | 7.83e-02 | 4.5 |
| 1e-02 | 8 | 16.6 | 12.7× | 7.56e-03 | 1.0 |
| 1e-03 | 23 | 123.5 | 1.7× | 9.42e-04 | 1.7 |
| 1e-04 | 30 | 225.0 | 0.9× | 4.69e-15 | 1.7 |
| 1e-06 | 30 | 225.0 | 0.9× | 4.69e-15 | 1.6 |

**Takeaway**: `eps=1e-2` gives 12.7× compression with <1% error — the sweet spot for pricing.
